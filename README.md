## Overview

Community Healing Zone is a monthly, volunteer-led outreach initiative I founded two years ago, focused on serving individuals experiencing homelessness in the DC area through clothing distribution, free haircuts, and community support.

As the organization grew, identifying and contacting potential church and community partners became increasingly time-consuming. Researching organizations, finding relevant contacts, drafting personalized outreach, and tracking activity required significant manual effort.

This project evaluated whether AI-assisted workflows could reduce outreach time while maintaining personalization and human oversight.

---

## Problem

The existing outreach process relied on manually:

* Researching potential partner organizations
* Identifying outreach contacts
* Reviewing websites and mission alignment
* Drafting personalized outreach messages
* Tracking outreach activity

This limited the number of organizations that could be contacted in a given work session.

The goal was to determine whether AI could improve throughput without sacrificing outreach quality.

---

## Approach

A manual baseline was established and timed across multiple outreach activities.

An AI-assisted workflow was then introduced to support:

* Research summarization
* Organization qualification
* Outreach signal extraction
* Personalized outreach drafting

Human review remained part of the process before any communication was sent.

---

## Results

### Manual Process

* Average time per lead: 7 minutes 48 seconds
* Throughput: 7.69 leads per hour

### AI-Assisted Process

* Average time per lead: 4 minutes 13 seconds
* Throughput: 14.23 leads per hour

### Impact

* Time reduction per lead: 45.94%
* Throughput increase: 84.98%
* 5-hour capacity increased from 38.46 leads to 71.15 leads
* Additional capacity: 32.69 leads per 5-hour work session

---

## Key Insight

The largest gain did not come from eliminating human involvement.

Instead, AI reduced the time spent synthesizing information and drafting outreach, allowing human attention to focus on judgment, relationship building, and final review.

Once drafting became faster, the next bottleneck shifted to website navigation and contact discovery.

## Lessons Learned

This project reinforced that AI is often most valuable when used to augment human workflows rather than replace them entirely.

The most effective implementation combined automation with human review, resulting in significant efficiency gains while maintaining outreach quality and contextual judgment.
